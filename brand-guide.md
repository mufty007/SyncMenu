# SyncMenu Brand Identity Guide

## 1. Brand Foundation

**Brand essence:** Menus that keep up with you.

**Personality:** Modern and clean at its core, with a playful, bold edge. SyncMenu should feel like the friendly, capable tool that a busy shop owner trusts — never corporate, never toy-like. Confident simplicity: crisp layouts, generous whitespace, one punchy hit of orange.

**Brand values:**
- **Instant** — everything should feel fast and live (the "sync" promise).
- **Approachable** — built for chicken shops and sandwich spots, not IT departments.
- **Polished** — the product makes small restaurants look professional; the brand must too.

**Tagline options:**
- "Your menu, live everywhere."
- "Update once. Everywhere, instantly."
- "Menus in sync."

## 2. Logo Concepts

**Concept A — The Sync Mark (recommended):** Wordmark "SyncMenu" in a bold geometric sans, with a custom icon: two rounded arrows forming a loop around a simplified menu/board shape. The loop communicates real-time sync; rounded corners keep it friendly. Icon works standalone as app icon/favicon.

**Concept B — The Flip Tile:** A rounded square tile (like a menu board) with the top half slightly offset or "flipping" — suggesting content changing live. Wordmark to the right.

**Concept C — Type-only:** Pure wordmark where "Sync" is set in orange and "Menu" in dark gray, with the "S" customized with a subtle circular-arrow terminal. Cleanest, cheapest to execute well.

**Logo rules:**
- Minimum clear space: height of the "S" on all sides.
- Never stretch, rotate, add shadows, or place on clashing colored backgrounds.
- One-color versions required: all-white (orange/dark backgrounds) and all-charcoal (light backgrounds).

## 3. Color Palette

### Primary
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Sync Orange | #FF6B2C | Hero color — CTAs, logo, highlights, active states |
| Deep Ember | #E4501A | Hover/pressed states, gradients with Sync Orange |

### Neutrals
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Charcoal | #1F2933 | Headlines, primary text |
| Slate | #52606D | Body text, secondary UI |
| Mist | #E4E7EB | Borders, dividers, subtle backgrounds |
| Cloud | #F5F7FA | Page/section backgrounds |
| White | #FFFFFF | Cards, primary surfaces |

### Functional accents (UI only, sparingly)
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Live Green | #22B573 | Screen online, success |
| Alert Red | #E5484D | Errors, screen offline |
| Sun Amber | #FFB020 | Warnings (e.g., nearing storage limit) |

**Usage ratio:** ~60% white/cloud, 30% neutrals, 10% orange. Orange is the spice, not the base.

## 4. Typography

**Body & UI: Poppins**
- Regular (400) body text, Medium (500) UI labels and buttons, SemiBold (600) subheadings.

**Display / Landing hero:** Bolder statement font from Google Fonts. Candidates that pair well with Poppins: Bricolage Grotesque, Space Grotesk, or Unbounded (Unbounded = most playful, Space Grotesk = most modern). Use only at large sizes in Bold/ExtraBold.

**Type scale (web):**
- Hero: 56–72px display font
- H1: 40px / H2: 28px / H3: 20px SemiBold
- Body: 16px / Small & labels: 14px Medium
- Line height: ~1.5 body, ~1.1 display

## 5. Visual Style

- **Shape language:** Rounded everything — 12–16px radius on cards and buttons, fully rounded pills for tags and status badges.
- **Iconography:** Outline icons, rounded caps and joins (Lucide or Phosphor), 2px stroke, Slate/Charcoal; orange reserved for active states.
- **Imagery:** Real photography of small restaurants — counters, menu boards glowing on TVs, owners updating prices on a phone. Warm lighting to harmonize with orange. No sterile corporate stock photos. Illustration (if used): simple, geometric, orange + neutrals only.
- **Motion:** Motion IS the brand. Quick, smooth UI transitions (150–250ms ease-out). Signature moment: content sync = subtle slide-up or orange pulse. Never bouncy or slow.
- **Screen motif:** Recurring rounded-rectangle "screen frame" containing menu content — used across landing page, social, onboarding.

## 6. Voice & Tone

- **Plainspoken:** "Change a price. It's on your screens in seconds." Not "Leverage real-time content distribution."
- **Encouraging, lightly playful:** Celebrate small wins in-product; skip emoji in marketing headlines.
- **Respectful of hustle:** Owners are busy. Copy is short, scannable, never condescending.

**Example copy:**
- Hero: "Your menu. Every screen. Always in sync."
- CTAs: "Start free trial" / "See it live"
- Empty state: "No screens yet — add one and we'll have you live in minutes."

## 7. Application Quick-Reference

- **Buttons:** Sync Orange fill, white Poppins Medium text, 12px radius, Deep Ember on hover.
- **Dashboard:** Cloud background, white cards, Charcoal text, orange only for primary actions and active nav.
- **Kiosk pairing screen:** Charcoal background, white logo, orange QR frame.
- **Favicon/app icon:** Standalone sync-loop icon, white on Sync Orange rounded square.
